Codex of Armenian Kings

A Crusader-Kings Tier-List Forge · House of Artsruni

Thirty crowned heads from the Orontid satraps to the last king of Cilicia, each rated on the five CK3 attributes. Portraits load from surviving coins, statues and manuscript miniatures; kings with no extant likeness keep a heraldic medallion. Drag or tap a sovereign into a tier, sort the pool, then export your ranking.

CK3 scale: ~8 average · 12+ skilled · 16+ great · 18–20 legendary. Ratings are interpretive — overrule them in your own tiering.
